What is REEF?

REEF Polling by i>clicker allows you to answer questions using your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch. All you have to do is tap the screen to provide your answer and then you will receive instant feedback. Compare your vote to the rest of the class to see how well you have done! After class, access the saved REEF Polling questions to study for a quiz or exam. All of the data is stored on the cloud so you can access it anywhere from any device.
